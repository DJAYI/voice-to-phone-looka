## 1. Key Service Features

By subscribing to any of our **PBX Cloud** plans, your business gets immediate access to advanced communication tools:

- **Full Mobility:** Use your extension on PC (softphone) and mobile devices.
- **Web Management:** Responsive PBX portal to manage your account from anywhere.
- **Automatic Attendant (IVR):** Interactive voice menu to route calls to the correct department.
- **Transfer and Forwarding:** Manage calls between extensions or forward them to external mobiles and landlines.

## 3. Plan Details: Basic, Pro and Premium

<p class="text-base text-slate-600 dark:text-slate-300">
  Below we detail each plan so you can compare features and choose the ideal one
  for your team.
</p>

### Basic Plan: Take the first step

The Basic Plan is the gateway to professional telephony. It includes a **Virtual Number (DID)** from your preferred city and allows up to 5 connected extensions. It's perfect for small offices that only need standard reception and call transfer functions.

### Pro Plan: The perfect balance

Our most popular plan. In addition to increasing capacity to 10 extensions and 8 simultaneous calls, it unlocks the **Multi-level IVR**. This allows creating complex menus (e.g., "Press 1 for Sales, 2 for Support") greatly improving the customer experience.

### Premium Plan: Corporate power

Designed for contact centers and companies with high call volume. With support for up to 30 extensions and 15 concurrent call channels, it ensures no customer hears a busy tone. Includes priority support with reduced response times.
